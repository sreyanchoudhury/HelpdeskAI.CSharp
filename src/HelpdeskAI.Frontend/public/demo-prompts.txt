HelpdeskAI — Demo Prompts
════════════════════════════════════════════════════════
A few ideas to get you started. Don't stop here though —
describe a real IT problem and see what happens!
════════════════════════════════════════════════════════

── Everyday IT Issues ───────────────────────────────────

"My laptop won't connect to the VPN. What should I do?"

"Outlook keeps crashing every time I try to open an attachment."

"I've been locked out of my work account since this morning —
 can you help me get back in?"

"My computer is running really slowly. Could it be a virus?"

"Teams calls keep dropping after about 5 minutes. Very frustrating."


── Raising a Support Ticket ─────────────────────────────

"I keep getting a Blue Screen of Death on startup.
 Can you raise a ticket for me?"

"Log a high-priority issue: our whole department lost access
 to SharePoint an hour ago."

"I've had a broken keyboard for two weeks and no one has
 followed up. Create a ticket and escalate it."


── Searching the Knowledge Base ─────────────────────────

"Is there a guide on setting up multi-factor authentication?"

"What's the standard process for onboarding a new employee's laptop?"

"Search the knowledge base for anything on printer setup."


── Try Uploading a File ──────────────────────────────────

Tap the paperclip and upload a screenshot of an error,
a crash log, or any work document, then ask:

"Can you analyse this and tell me what the issue is?"

"Add a summary of this to the knowledge base."

"Create a ticket based on what you see in this file."


── Push It Further ───────────────────────────────────────

"My entire team can't access Salesforce. This is urgent —
 diagnose the issue, create a P1 ticket, and tell me
 who I should escalate to."

"I'm getting error code 0x80070002 when Windows updates.
 Walk me through fixing it step by step."

"We had a security incident — someone clicked a phishing link
 on our finance team's machine. What do we do?"


── Just Have a Conversation ──────────────────────────────

The agent keeps context throughout the session.
Try following up on an earlier message, asking for
clarification, or changing direction mid-conversation.
